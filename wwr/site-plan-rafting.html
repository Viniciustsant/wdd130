Target Audience

Who are the target customers?

Our target customers are adventure seekers, outdoor enthusiasts, and families seeking thrilling water-based experiences. They typically fall within the age range of 18-45, have a moderate to comfortable financial situation, and are passionate about outdoor activities such as rafting, kayaking, camping, and hiking.

What do they want?

They want exciting and memorable experiences in natural settings, opportunities to challenge themselves physically and mentally, and the chance to bond with friends or family while enjoying outdoor adventures. They seek adrenaline-filled activities that provide a break from routine and create lasting memories.

What do they expect to find on the website?

They expect to find comprehensive information about the various rafting trips and packages offered, including difficulty levels, safety measures, equipment provided, locations, river conditions, expertise of guides, booking procedures, pricing transparency, and testimonials from previous customers.
