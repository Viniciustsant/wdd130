History

Gravity Rafting has been providing thrilling white water rafting adventures for over a decade. Founded in 2010 by outdoor enthusiast Vinicius Santiago, the company started with a single raft and a passion for exploring the untamed rivers of the world.

Over the years, Gravity Rafting has grown into one of the premier rafting companies in the region, offering a range of guided trips for adventurers of all skill levels. Our commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental stewardship has earned us a reputation for excellence in the industry.

As we look back on our journey, we're grateful for the countless memories made on the river and the friendships forged along the way. We're excited to continue sharing the thrill of white water rafting with you for many years to come.
